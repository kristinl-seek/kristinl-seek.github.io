About Me

Mom | Guitars | Poetry | Nature

Single mom, part-time guitar hero, and full-time adventurer. When I'm not developing engaging e-Learning content, you can find me juggling my kids schedules and pretending to be a rock star, I’m always on the lookout for the next great outdoor escapade. I've published a poetry book, and I'm pretty sure my daughter has a higher Duolingo day streak for learning Turkish. Whether it’s shredding a riff or shredding a mountain, I’m happiest when I’m having fun with my family. Let’s connect over virtual coffee, good music, or a great hike!
